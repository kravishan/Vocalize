Details

Welcome to our research project! This instruction page provides guidance on participating in our study. Below is a to-do list to guide you through the process:

This research aims to gather valuable insights into restaurant reviews using two methods:

  • Google Map Method: Provide your feedback using the Google Map application.
  • LLM Method: Share your thoughts using the Vocalizer LLM application.

Please complete the tasks in the to-do list and follow the provided links to access the applications and Google forms.

What We're Trying to Do:

We're on an exciting journey to see how smart computer programs can change how we make reviews. Our main goal is to find out how these computer programs, known as AI, can make reviews better and cover more things. In this study, we're trying a new way to get reviewsâ€”from your voice, not just typing. This new idea is meant to make giving reviews more like talking to a friend or someone you know well, and we improve your review using AI.

Picture telling someone about a restaurant in the same way you'd tell a friend. That's what we want to capture with this new voice method. It's different from the usual way of typing words on a screen.

How Long We Need Your Reviews:

We're looking to collect reviews over a period of 6 days. We've split everyone into two groups: one group will use the LLM application first, while the other will start with the Google Map Application. You'll use one application for reviewing three specific restaurants and the other application for the remaining three.

Make sure to fill out the Google form related to the application you used after completing the reviews for the first three restaurants according to your group.

Data Usage Policy:

We collect your location data for research. Your privacy is important. We only use your data for our study, and we delete it when we finish. We don't share it or use it for anything else. If you have questions, ask us.
